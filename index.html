Ruby is a language for people who like things to make sense. It uses language very similar to every day usage, rather that adding a ton of punctuation to trip you up! If you want to more about Ruby straight from the Ruby community, click here.

JavaScript

JavaScript is a language for people who have an obsession with detail. This language requires very precise grammar in order to ensure function. One missing semicolon has been known to take years off of a programmer's life. But if you enjoy hunting for characters lost in a sea of characters, then this is the language for you.
